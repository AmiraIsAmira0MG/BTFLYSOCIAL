Why BTFLY

Built differently.

🔓

Truly decentralised

No single company owns BTFLY. Built on open protocols so your account and content live outside any one server's control.

🛡️

Privacy by default

Your data belongs to you. No surveillance advertising, no data brokering, no shadow profiles. Privacy is built in, not bolted on.

🌐

Federated & open

Connect across the open social web. BTFLY talks to the broader fediverse so you're never locked into one platform or walled garden.

✂️

No algorithmic feed

See what you choose to see — in chronological order. No black-box ranking designed to maximise your time on screen.

🏡

Communities you own

Create and moderate your own spaces with rules that reflect your community's values — not a corporation's Terms of Service.

⚡

Fast & lightweight

No bloated tracking scripts or ad infrastructure. BTFLY is lean, fast, and designed to work well on any device or connection.
